About

A simple stay, done well

We're in Dasol (2411), right on the beach. Guests come back for the clear water, clean rooms, and people who still smile when you arrive late after holiday traffic.

Need Colibra Island tips? Ask the caretaker. There's a sari-sari nearby if you need snacks. Ground-floor rooms are easiest if you prefer fewer stairs.

  • Private CR on all rooms except kubo
  • Free cottage with every room
